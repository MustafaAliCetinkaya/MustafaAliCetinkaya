Step-by-step Instructions

Step 1

Using a straightedge, draw any two chords of the circle. For greatest accuracy, avoid chords that are nearly parallel.

Step 2

Construct the perpendicular bisector of one of the chords using the method described in Constructing a perpendicular bisector of a line segment

Step 3

Repeat for the other chord

Step 4

The point where the two lines intersect is the center C of the circle.
